Influencers

  1. Home
  2. Influencers

Sometimes it may be overlooked but influencers' Wikipedia articles may be crucial in their image. With top influencers receiving over 5000 daily views on their articles it really speaks for itself how critical Wikipedia is. Lettoir ensures a detailed and accurate article about them and their lives, provided they are notable of course. This includes an infobox that is pretty much consistent among all major influencers, a detailed biography and really any important detail that connects to them or their fans.

In most cases, high level influencers who don't have a Wikipedia page yet have had their fans (or sometimes themselves) try and create one. Due to inexperience on Wikipedia, without proper citation the article usually gets deleted and protected from any non-experienced editor to try and recreate it. This usually limits it to extended-confirmed users eligible to edit it. This is where we come in and help straighten out this situation by creating the article appropriately to make it encyclopedic and make sure it is a proper Wikipedia article.
