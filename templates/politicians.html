It is well known politicians love to use Wikipedia to their advantage. Over and over we have seen cases of politicians going ahead and editing sections of their Wikipedia page to their advantage. And who can blame them, who wouldn't in their shoes. The conflict arises when this is spotted and gets reported by the media. This is where, as Wiki Royale we come in. We create and modify pages of politicians making sure it is encyclopedic thus conforming to Wikipedia's rules. We take great effort in ensuring we cite high quality sources in ensuring the content of the article is up to the required standard. Not only this but also we translate the pages for politicians who appeal more to their voters in different languages as well as adding images and an accurate, neat infobox.

We ensure the page stays neutral and unbiased. This goes both ways as we ensure that we only make edits that are acceptable to Wikipedia as well as ensure that no malicious editors who try and make vandalism edits to the detriment of our clients.
